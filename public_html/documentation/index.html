Executive Summary

MissionCitizen, will provide a well-rounded service for those looking to give back to their communities. Organizations and representatives will have the opportunity to share their volunteer opportunities with others. They will have options to create and manage events, track volunteer hours and communicate with volunteers. New users will have the opportunity to explore volunteer events and read testimonials about successful events without having to register. Users will have the option to create a profile to track future events. Administrators of the site will validate all users and enforce community guidelines to protect the user experience. By addressing the needs of each user, the app will simplify the process of finding volunteer opportunities and promoting community involvement.
